Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

General Questions

How can I register on the website?

To register, simply go to the registration page and fill out the required information such as your username, email, and password.

How do I earn money?

You can earn money by completing tasks, referring friends, or any other methods provided on the platform.

Deposit & Withdrawal

How can I deposit funds?

Go to the Deposit page, select your preferred payment method, and enter the amount you wish to deposit.

How do I withdraw my earnings?

Navigate to the Withdrawal page, select your withdrawal method, and enter the amount you wish to withdraw.
